**Learned:**
- MLflow vocabulary: Experiment (named group of runs), Run (one execution), Param (input/hyperparameter), Metric (output measurement), Artifact (a file, e.g. the model). The logged model bundle (MLmodel + model.pkl + env) is what makes it re-loadable and is the basis of the Model Registry.
- venv is per-project (isolated deps); `source .venv/bin/activate` -> python/pip point at that project's interpreter. macOS/Homebrew has no bare `python`; `python3` = newest (3.14), use `python3.12` explicitly or work inside the venv.
