**Flashcard round score:** ~6.5/10 avg. Locked Trapping Rain Water (9/10, why moving the shorter side is safe). Three K8s mechanism items to firm up (concepts solid, precise mechanism slips):
- Rolling update = the Deployment scales the NEW ReplicaSet up gradually while scaling the OLD one down gradually (overlap = zero downtime); old kept at 0 for rollback.
- Deployment selector vs template labels: if they don't match, Kubernetes REJECTS the Deployment at apply time (validation error), because the selector defines which pods the Deployment manages. (Load balancing is the Service's concern, separate.)
- Delete a pod when desired=3: the ReplicaSet controller detects actual<desired and RECREATES a replacement (self-healing). Deletion ordering (StatefulSet highest-ordinal-first) is a scale-DOWN concept, different action.
